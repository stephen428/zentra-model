Acquisitions

For each deal set the revenue & EBITDA, then the deal structure: what % is paid upfront and what % is deferred as an earnout. The model checks whether cash generated from the acquired business can service the earnout payments.

Upfront payments Earnout payments Acquired EBITDA generated Cumulative net cash (line)

Upfront = paid at acquisition date. Earnout = deferred consideration paid annually over the earnout period. Acquired EBITDA = operating cash generated by the acquired business (before integration savings). Net = cumulative EBITDA less all consideration paid.
